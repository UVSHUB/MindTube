Why We Built PilotAI

The Problem

Students are overwhelmed by hours of video lectures and struggle to extract the key information needed for exams. Traditional note-taking can't keep up with the pace of online learning.

The Solution

We built an intelligent learning companion that watches videos for you, summarizes key concepts, and helps you study smarter, not harder. It's like having a personal tutor available 24/7.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind PilotAI?

We are a small, independent team of engineers, filmmakers, and data scientists. We're self-funded and driven by a passion for better storytelling tools.

How do you handle data privacy?

Privacy is a core value. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your private scripts to train our public models without your explicit opt-in.
