Frequently Asked Questions

How does the semantic analysis work?

We use a custom-trained transformer model that has been fine-tuned on millions of high-performing video scripts. It understands context, humor, and pacing better than generic LLMs.

Is it completely free?

Yes. PilotAI is currently in open beta and is free for all creators. We want to empower as many storytellers as possible.

Can I upload video files directly?

Currently, we support YouTube links and text scripts. Direct video file upload is coming in the next release.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your scripts and video data are processed in a secure, private environment and are never used to train our public models without your explicit permission.
